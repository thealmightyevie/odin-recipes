Pecan Pie

Description

This recipe is for a wonderfully rich Southern pecan pie! It's simple, delicious and can be put together in under an hour with basic ingredients. What more could you ask for?

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon cold water
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups chopped pecans
  • 9 inch unbaked pie shell
