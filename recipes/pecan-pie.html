Pecan Pie

Description

This recipe is for a wonderfully rich Southern pecan pie! It's simple, delicious and can be put together in under an hour with basic ingredients. What more could you ask for?

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon cold water
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups chopped pecans
  • 9 inch unbaked pie shell

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees Celsius.
  2. Combine sugar, corn syrup, butter, water, and cornstarch in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a full boil, and remove from heat.
  3. In a large bowl, beat eggs until frothy. Gradually beat in cooked syrup mixture
  4. Stir in salt, vanilla, and pecans. Pour into pie shell.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is set, 45 to 50 minutes.
