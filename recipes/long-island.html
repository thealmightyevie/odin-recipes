The REAL Long Island Iced Tea

Description

This fantastic cocktail can be made many ways, however this recipe is guaranteed to be the ultimate version. Despite the fact that it contains Long Island and tea in its name, its not related to either. In fact its better referred to as a "suicide soda"; a concoction of every drink poured into one cup. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 fluid ounce vodka
  • 1/2 fluid ounce rum
  • 1/2 fluid ounce gin
  • 1/2 fluid ounce tequila
  • 1/2 fluid ounce triple sec (orange-flavoured liqueur)
  • 1 fluid ounce sweet and sour mix
  • 1 fluid ounce cola, or to taste
  • 1 lemon slice

Steps

  1. Fill cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Pour vodka, rum, gin, tequila, triple sec, and sour mix over ice; cover and shake.
  3. Pour cocktail into a Collins or hurricane glass.
  4. Top with splash of cola for colour.
  5. Garnish with a lemon slice.
